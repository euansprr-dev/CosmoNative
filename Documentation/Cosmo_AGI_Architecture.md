# THE VISION

CosmoOS is not an app. It is a **Personal AGI** — an artificial general intelligence trained on *you*.

While other apps track isolated metrics, CosmoOS captures the **complete signal of your existence** and transforms it into actionable self-knowledge through cross-dimensional correlation analysis.

**The result:** A system that feels telepathic — surfacing insights you didn't know existed, predicting your optimal performance windows, and revealing the hidden causality between your physiology, psychology, behavior, and creative output.
